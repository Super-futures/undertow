Three registers are tracked simultaneously at each chokepoint. Flow reflects actual transit volume and physical constraint (0 = closed, 100 = fully open). Market reflects price and shipping signals as a forward-looking proxy. Felt reflects collective public sentiment derived from news volume and narrative spread. The gap between Flow and Felt is the primary signal. When significant, the system shows its configuration and the channel through which it is most likely to become visible in daily life.
