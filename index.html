Three registers are tracked simultaneously at each chokepoint. Flow reflects actual transit volume and physical constraint (0 = closed, 100 = fully open). Market reflects price and war-risk insurance signals as a forward-looking proxy — markets price expected disruption before it fully materialises. Felt reflects collective public sentiment derived from news volume and narrative spread — typically the slowest of the three to move. The gap between Flow and Felt is the primary signal: when physical reality is severe but collective awareness has not yet registered it, the system surfaces a comparable historical moment.
